Our Story

Founded with a vision to bridge the educational gap in Zambia, Elevate Education Network started as a small initiative focused on teacher support. We quickly realized that the challenges were systemic and required a technological leap.

By integrating advanced data analytics and localized AI models, we are transforming from a local support group into a nationwide network that provides real-time insights into student performance and teacher impact.
